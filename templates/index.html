About Us

A rumor/rumour is basically a form of a statement whose legitimacy is not yet confirmed.

Rumour gives birth to the spread of misinformation in public. These days we get most of the information about what's happening around the world from various online social media websites. Twitter is one of the most popular platforms, where people tweet about various events. One of the major problem we all face on these social media platforms is “RUMOURS”. There is no authenticity regarding these tweets.

Tweets

500 million tweets are sent each day, that equates to 5.787 tweets per second

We detect probability of a tweet being a rumour

Each and every tweet has various parametes based on which we can calculate the probability of that tweet being a rumour.

We have used various Natural Language Processing techniques to determine the probability value.
